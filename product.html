The Platform

A structured environment where litigation meets capital—designed for control, clarity, and efficiency on both sides.

For Law Firms

Structured Case Presentation

Submit cases once through a standardized intake process. No more rewriting decks for every funder. Your case is presented consistently, professionally, and on your terms.

  • Present to multiple funders without redundant work

For Funders

Staged Evaluation Process

Review cases through a structured, multi-stage process designed for efficient diligence. See only what matters at each stage—no wasted time on misaligned opportunities.

  • Filter cases by your criteria before deep review
  • Staged access reveals detail as interest progresses
  • Clear process reduces back-and-forth
