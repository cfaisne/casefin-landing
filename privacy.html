Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

CaseFin ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: We collect information you provide directly, including account registration details, case information, documents, and communications through the Platform.

Automatically Collected Information: We may collect certain information automatically, including IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Platform
  • Process and facilitate information sharing between users
  • Communicate with you about your account and the Platform
  • Improve and optimize the Platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users as directed by you through the Platform's functionality
  • With service providers who assist in operating the Platform
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to legal process
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may also retain information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@casefin.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on the Platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@casefin.com.

CaseFin provides software infrastructure only and does not act as a broker, marketplace operator, or advisor in connection with litigation funding transactions.

CaseFin does not participate in funding decisions, negotiate transaction terms, recommend counterparties, or structure funding arrangements. All decisions regarding litigation funding are made independently by third-party participants.

Nothing on this platform constitutes an offer, solicitation, recommendation, or endorsement of any transaction or legal strategy. Case outcomes, funding availability, and economic terms are inherently uncertain and are not guaranteed.

Users are solely responsible for obtaining their own legal, financial, and regulatory advice before entering into any litigation funding arrangement. Use of CaseFin is subject to applicable laws, professional rules, and ethical obligations.

CaseFin is designed to support information transparency and workflow efficiency, not to influence or replace independent professional judgment. CaseFin does not evaluate or endorse cases. Information is presented as provided by users.
